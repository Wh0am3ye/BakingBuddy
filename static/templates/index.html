Your best friend for baking.

Don't you hate it when you find a recipe that you love the look of but all the units need converting?

How about when you're looking to make a recipe for a party or an intimate dinner and all of the recipes you find are for the wrong number of people?

That's where BakingBuddy comes in.

With BakingBuddy you can copy in your recipe and scale it up or down for the exact number of people you need to serve.

You can also plug in the ingredients in your recipe and have them converted to the units of your preference.

No more constantly searching for how to convert various cup measures into grams, you can do it all in one place.

BakingBuddy will save each of your ingredients so you can then copy them into the measurement converter to increase or decrease the servings in the weights you're used to using.
